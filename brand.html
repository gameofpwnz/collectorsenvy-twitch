About Our Brand

Collector's Envy is a dedicated community for esports fans who buy, sell, trade, and discover rare memorabilia and jerseys. Our brand represents passion, authenticity, and the thrill of the hunt for that perfect piece of esports history.

Usage Guidelines

Clear Space

Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "C" in Collector's. Never crowd the logo with other elements.

Minimum Size

Never use the logo smaller than 40px in height for digital applications or 0.5 inches for print to maintain legibility.

Background Usage

Use the primary logo on light backgrounds and the reverse logo on dark backgrounds. Ensure sufficient contrast.

Color Modifications

Use whatever color combinations that you want. The ones on this website are just examples.

DO

  • Use official logo files from the brand folder
  • Maintain original logo proportions
  • Ensure proper contrast with backgrounds
  • Keep logos crisp and clear

DON'T

  • Stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
  • Use low-resolution or pixelated versions
  • Place logo on busy backgrounds
  • Recreate or redraw the logo
